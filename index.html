Our Legacy

Govind Bhawan is one of the oldest and most prestigious hostels at IIT Roorkee. Home to students across disciplines, we pride ourselves on academic excellence, sporting achievements, and a vibrant cultural ecosystem that shapes future leaders.

Our residents have gone on to become scientists, entrepreneurs, civil servants, and artists — carrying forward the spirit of Govind Bhawan to every corner of the globe.

Booking Rules & Guidelines

  • Advance booking of minimum 2 days required. Walk-in subject to availability.
  • Payment at the hostel office before check-in. Cash & UPI accepted.
  • Guests must carry valid government ID (Aadhar / Passport / Voter ID).
  • No cooking in rooms. Mess food available for guests at nominal charges.
  • Maximum stay of 5 consecutive nights per booking.
  • Residents are responsible for their guests' conduct at all times.
